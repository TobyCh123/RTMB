About RTMB

RTMB started as a WhatsApp group chat and has now evolved into this, a drink tracking app designed for social drink tracking providing fun facts about you and your friends drink habits.

How It Works

Getting started is simple:

  • Create an account in the mobile app or continue as a guest
  • Start logging drinks as you enjoy them
  • Join or create a group with friends
  • Watch as your group tracks it's drink journey
  • Compare and share statistics together

Responsible Drinking

We believe in celebrating every drink culture responsibly. RTMB is designed to track and share your experiences, not to encourage excessive consumption. Please always drink responsibly and never drink and drive.
