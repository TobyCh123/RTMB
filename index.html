About Road To A Million Beers

Road To A Million Beers started as a WhatsApp group chat and has now evolved into this, a beer tracking app designed for social beer tracking providing fun facts about you and your friends drinking.

How It Works

Getting started is simple:

  • Create an account or sign in as a guest
  • Start logging beers as you enjoy them
  • Join or create a group with friends
  • Watch as your group tracks it's beer journey
  • Compare and share statistics together

Responsible Drinking

We believe in celebrating beer culture responsibly. Road to a Million is designed to track and share your experiences, not to encourage excessive consumption. Please always drink responsibly and never drink and drive.
